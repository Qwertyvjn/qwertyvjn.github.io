IPCC Carbon Counting — Methodology

1 & 2. The Foundation

Carbon Counting quantifies greenhouse gases (CO₂e) emitted directly or indirectly. The IPCC provides the scientific guidelines used worldwide.

The GHG Protocol turns IPCC science into practical rules.

3. Why It Matters

You cannot manage what you do not measure. Carbon counting finds hotspots so engineers can act.
