5. Scope and Tier Breakdown

The three Scopes define where emissions occur. Click below to explore.

Scope 1: Direct Emissions

Emissions from sources you own/control — company vehicles, fuel combustion, process emissions.

Scope 2: Purchased Energy

Indirect emissions from electricity, heating, cooling you buy.

Scope 3: Value Chain

Everything else — supply chain, travel, waste, investments. Usually the largest.

Example Calculation

100 kWh × 0.71 kg CO₂/kWh = 71 kg CO₂e (Scope 2 - Indonesia grid)
